Privacy Policy

GoCalGo — Pokemon Go Events Calendar
Last updated: 22 March 2026

Overview

GoCalGo is a free mobile application that displays Pokemon Go event schedules. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Collect

GoCalGo collects the minimum data necessary to deliver its core features:

  • Device token: A Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) registration token is generated by your device. This token is used solely to deliver push notifications you have opted into. It is not linked to your identity.
  • Event flags: When you flag an event for notifications, the event ID and your device token are sent to our server so we can schedule a reminder.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No contacts, photos, or other device data

How We Use Your Data

Device tokens and event flags are used exclusively to send you push notifications about events you have flagged. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

Data Storage and Security

Device tokens and event flags are stored on our servers and transmitted securely. Data is retained only as long as needed to deliver notifications. If you uninstall the app, your device token becomes invalid and associated data is eventually removed.

Third-Party Services

GoCalGo uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google): Used to deliver push notifications. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

GoCalGo does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or collection of personal information from any user.

Your Choices

  • You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.
  • You can unflag events in the app to stop receiving reminders for them.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to stop all data collection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@gocalgo.app.
